How it works

Our service allows you to delete your user data from our system by providing your email address. Once you enter your email address and submit the form, our system will delete all data associated with that email address, including:

  • Your location history
  • Your search history
  • Your saved locations
  • Your user preferences

Data types

Our system saves the following types of data:

  • Location history
  • Search history
  • Saved locations
  • User preferences

Instructions

  1. Enter your email address in the form below
  2. Click the "Delete My Data" button
  3. Check your email for a confirmation message
  4. Click the link in the confirmation message to confirm the deletion of your data
